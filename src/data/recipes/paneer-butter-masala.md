A classic North Indian vegetarian dish that's rich, creamy, and absolutely delicious. Perfect with naan or rice!

## Ingredients

### For the Gravy:

- 4 large tomatoes, chopped
- 2 onions, chopped
- 1 inch ginger
- 4-5 garlic cloves
- 2 green chilies
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1/2 cup fresh cream
- Salt to taste

### For Paneer:

- 250g paneer, cubed
- 1 tbsp oil for frying

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the base**: Blanch tomatoes, onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Blend into a smooth puree.

2. **Cook the gravy**: Heat butter in a pan, add the puree, and cook for 10-15 minutes until oil separates.

3. **Add spices**: Add red chili powder, garam masala, salt, and kasuri methi. Mix well.

4. **Add paneer**: Gently add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.

5. **Finish**: Add fresh cream, mix gently, and cook for 2 more minutes.

## Tips

- Don't overcook paneer or it will become rubbery
- Kasuri methi adds a wonderful aroma - don't skip it!
- For a richer taste, add a tablespoon of cashew paste to the gravy

Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice!
